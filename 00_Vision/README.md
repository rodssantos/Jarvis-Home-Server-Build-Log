# 🟣 FYNO — Your Private Home AI

## 🎯 Overview

FYNO is a private, local-first AI system designed to live inside your home.

It combines artificial intelligence, personal data, and home infrastructure into a single system that helps you manage, understand, and protect your digital life.

---

## 🧬 Meaning

**FYNO = Fully Yours. Naturally Optimized.**

* Fully Yours → your data, your memories, your control
* Naturally Optimized → adapts, learns, and improves over time

---

## 🧠 Vision

To build a home AI system that is:

* Private by design
* Centered around personal data and memories
* Accessible from any device
* Modular and scalable
* Capable of evolving into a real-world product

---

## 📸 Core Focus — Personal Memory System

FYNO is designed to become the center of your digital life.

This includes:

* Secure photo and video backup
* Local media storage (self-hosted)
* Intelligent search across photos
* Timeline-based memory organization
* Future AI features:

  * face recognition
  * event grouping
  * natural language queries (e.g., "show me last Christmas")

Your memories remain private, stored locally, and always accessible.

---

## 🏠 What FYNO Is

FYNO is a **home intelligence system** composed of:

* Local AI models (via Ollama)
* Web-based interface (Open WebUI)
* Photo and media system (Immich or similar)
* Voice interaction (future)
* Home automation integration
* Personal knowledge base

---

## ❌ What FYNO Is Not

* Not a cloud AI assistant
* Not dependent on external AI providers
* Not a generic smart home hub

---

## 🔑 Core Principles
